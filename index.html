About Me

Meow! I'm Catboy Carter. You can call me Carter. I'm an orange cat from the French countryside. With my one precious braincell, I want to help my fellow stray oranges and non-oranges insert themselves into the cat distribution system and find a human home that they can happily purr in for the rest of at least one of their nine lives!

Chimken Nuggets:
• Favorite activity: Watching the birds outside (certainly not running after them), sleeping on my mom's head.
• Favorite food: Lasagna
• Favorite toy: New cardboxes that arrive when my mom orders delivery.

How It Works

Follow

What do we do on social media when we want to be noticed? The same principle applies here. Except you have to use your four paws and walk. Or trot. Or gallop. Do what you have to do to lasso your human! Or, most likely, get lasso-ed. It works either way!

Meow & Purr

Some humans like my mom have bad eyes and have to wear another pair on top of theirs. So don't be shy and let out your best meows! When the human finally pets you, purr happily to tell them thanks. If they still don't take you home after this, go back to step 1 and repeat! Don't give up!

Be Happy

Congratulations! You have successfully infiltrated the cat distribution system and distributed yourself to your new family! Now you get to enjoy a life of full of love... Your new life is but a dream!
